    Trajectory

    Welcome to the RPS calculator. This tool will guide you through the process of creating your own renewable energy policy and testing this policy’s efficacy. Your policy will be considered successful if the cost of the total amount of renewable energy you require is less than the maximum cost you allow.

    The first step in this process is to determine the proportion of renewable energy your state will require in each year, termed the RPS Trajectory. The graph below illustrates your state’s RPS trajectory. You are free to adjust this policy, but keep in mind the choices you make about the trajectory will affect the overall cost of your policy.

    Your state’s trajectory is represented by the dotted line. To create your own RPS schedule, click and drag the line to adjust the blue area. If you would rather type in your values, toggle-on the user input boxes by clicking “type” on top of the graph. If you would like to require your state to procure more than 50% of its energy from renewable sources, change the graph’s scale from 50% to 100% by clicking “100” on top of the graph.
