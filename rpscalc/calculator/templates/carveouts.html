    Carveouts

    Now that you have chosen your state’s RPS trajectory, you have the opportunity to define which technologies your state will use to meet its requirements. Some states establish these technology requirements by establishing a trajectory, or carve-out, for each technology they wish to promote. The RPS Calculator only allows you to set minimum requirements for wind and solar power as a percentage of each years RPS requirement (i.e. a percentage of the requirement you set on the previous screen). The graph below illustrates your state’s minimum technology requirements for wind and solar power. Keep in mind that the technologies you require will affect the cost of the policy.

    Your state’s current carve-outs are displayed in the default view. To adjust or create your own minimum technology requirements, click and drag the blue area and yellow areas. Yellow represents the percentage of your RPS that must be fulfilled with solar energy, while blue represents wind power. If you would rather type in your values, toggle-on the user input boxes by clicking “type” on top of the graph. If you would like to require your state to procure more than 50% of its energy from renewable sources, change the graph’s scale from 50% to 100% by clicking “100” on top of the graph.
