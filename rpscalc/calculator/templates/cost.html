You have now established the fundamental design of your RPS. On this page you will test the efficacy of your policy. The blue region represents the cost of the mean Renewable Energy Credit (REC), in dollars per megawatt-hour, calculated based on the trajectories and assumptions you made. The orange region represents the REC costs, in dollars per megawatt-hour, prohibited by the cost cap you imposed. Any cross-hatched region that appears represents time periods in which you have ‘broken’ your cost cap.
